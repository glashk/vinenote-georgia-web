## 6️⃣ Notes for App Review (English)

**App Review Notes:**

VineNote Georgia is a data management app for Georgian winemakers. The app allows users to record and track information about their vineyards, harvests, qvevri operations, and wine batches.

**Key Points:**
- The app works completely offline. Users can record data without internet connectivity.
- No in-app purchases or payments are required. The app is free to use.
- No user tracking or analytics are collected beyond basic app functionality.
- No social features or user-generated content sharing.
- The app is designed for business use by winemakers to manage their operations.

The app stores user-entered data locally and syncs to cloud storage when online. All data is user-generated content related to winemaking operations.

The app interface is available in both Georgian and English languages to serve the local Georgian winemaking community.
